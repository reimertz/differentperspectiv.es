Different Perspectives

interactive demo using google cardboard to experience how bullying look, feel and sound.

learn moredownload prototype

School start. I remember the anxiety of standing there in the morning, looking out over the school yard. Now it begins. The glances. The harrasment and intimidations. The exclusion.

This is a normal day for 60 000 children in Sweden. This needs to stop.

If children understood how much it hurts, they would probably stop. If children knew how to intervene, they probably would.

This is why we created Different Perspectives.
